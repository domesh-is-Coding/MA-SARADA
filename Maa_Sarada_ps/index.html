About Us

Ma Sarada Public School was formed in 1986 with KG1 and is run by Ma Sarada Sewa Samiti, a sister concern of Sri Ramakrishna Seva Mandal Bhilai. It was formed under the guidance and leadership of Bramhleen Swami Atmanand Ji, then secretary of Ramakrishna Mission Vivekananda Ashram Jaipur. Now it is run under the guidance of the secretary of Ramakrishna Mission Vivekananda Ashram Jaipur, who is the patron of the society.

The school imparts quality education based on the principles of Swami Vivekananda, focusing on man-making and character-building education. This institution is not a commercial institution; it operates on a no-profit-no-loss basis. The school is affiliated with CBSE up to class 12 in all streams. It is spread over two acres of land with green gardens and well-equipped laboratories and classrooms, adhering to CBSE norms.
